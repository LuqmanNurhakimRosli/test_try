About us

Your #1 Choice for Oil and Gas Trading

Established in 2024, Ping Energy Marketing Limited (“PEML”) is a trading company that operates under the umbrella of DNeX Energy and has full focus on energy commodity trading business, leveraging on existing relationship of DNeX Group in connecting the Supplier and End User.
