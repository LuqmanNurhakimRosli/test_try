About us

Full focus on energy commodities trading business

Established in 2024, Ping Energy Marketing Limited (“PEML”) is a trading company that operates under the umbrella of DNeX Energy and has full focus on energy commodities trading business, leveraging on existing relationship of DNeX Group in connecting the Supplier and End User.

We are committed to exceptional energy solutions to our valued customers delivering.

What we do

At PEML, we value our customers by ensuring that only the finest energy commodities are tailored and delivered to meet their demands through our trading service, supporting them in achieving their goals in the ever-evolving industry.

We are dedicated to offering high quality oil and gas petrochemical products with a wide array of options for our customers to choose from.
