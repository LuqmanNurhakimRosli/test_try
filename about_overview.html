About us

Full focus on energy commodities trading business

Established in 2024, Ping Energy Marketing Limited (“PEML”) is a trading company that operates under DNeX Energy, focusing on energy commodities trading business, leveraging on existing relationship of DNeX Group in connecting the Supplier and End User.

What we do

At PEML, we value our customers by ensuring that energy commodities are tailored and delivered to meet their demands through our trading service, by offering high quality oil and gas products with a wide array of options for our valued customers to choose from.
